山鬼情书之零

  • 2018-Dec-12 |
  • 诗歌

匹夫说我生来骄傲，

I’m proud of my husband.

不肯半分折腰，

Unwilling to half split up.

亦不能半分低头。

You can not half bow.

我将送喜欢的人远走,

I will go away the people I like.

此后情书写给山鬼,

After that, the love letter was written to the Mountain Ghost.

心事寄于西风，

My mind is in the west wind.
